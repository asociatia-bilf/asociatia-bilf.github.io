Our services

We provide you with nursing, nursing and supervision at your home seven days a week

Meal service*

  • Daily choice from a changing menu
  • Coolers delivered
  • You can compose your meal yourself (first, main, dessert)
  • From one to seven days a week

 

*Our volunteers deliver the meals at your house to your kitchen.

Laundry & Ironing Service

Our external laundry will pick up the laundry at your home and rewash and fold it back after about 3 working days. You can use the laundry service as often as you wish.

Day activities

Daily spending is always in a group. You stay in a homey and stimulating environment and we offer a program that suits you.
